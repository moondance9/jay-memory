# Project: VASP Strategy

## Project ID
vasp_strategy

## Status
Active

## Executive Summary
GMT faces constraints in directly acquiring or establishing VASP-related entities due to major shareholder suitability issues. The strategy requires careful comparison among direct application, acquisition, minority participation, affiliate participation, and alternative non-VASP structures.

## Key Workstreams
1. Direct VASP application
2. VASP acquisition
3. Majority shareholder suitability analysis
4. ISMS / FIU process
5. Non-VASP stablecoin payment structures

## Risks
- Major shareholder suitability
- ISMS cost and timeline
- Legal uncertainty after regulatory amendments
- Operational burden after acquisition
