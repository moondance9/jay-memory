# Risks: Circle CMP

## High
- CPN may not be feasible for Korean legal entity under current Korean regulatory interpretation.
- PR language may be misread as GMT conducting VASP-like activity.
- Refund flow involving USDC may create Electronic Financial Transaction Act or foreign exchange interpretation issues if poorly structured.

## Medium
- Circle may prefer global standardized product flows that do not perfectly fit Korea-specific prepaid card / foreign visitor use case.
- Legal counsel views may differ from Circle legal team or regulator interpretation.
- Product naming and role descriptions may create unintended regulatory signals.

## Mitigation
- Keep GMT role as KRW settlement, PG, prepaid, merchant/acquirer infrastructure.
- Avoid saying GMT receives, exchanges, transfers, or custodies crypto for users.
- Use foreign visitor / non-resident use case carefully.
- Separate CPN, Managed Payments, and Agentic Payment in all internal/external communication.
