## Executive Summary
Circle CPN appears difficult for a Korean entity under the current regulatory interpretation. Managed Payments and Agentic Payment remain the more realistic cooperation tracks. GMT should position itself as a KRW settlement, PG, prepaid, and merchant infrastructure partner, not as an unlicensed VASP.

## Strategic Direction
- Prioritize Managed Payments and Agentic Payment over CPN.
- Avoid messaging that GMT directly performs crypto exchange, custody, or unlicensed transfer.
- Frame GMT as regulated KRW payment, prepaid, settlement, and merchant infrastructure.
- Keep PR language conservative and aligned with Korean regulation.

## Key Workstreams
1. Managed Payments
2. Agentic Payment / Nano Payment
3. PR and messaging
4. Legal / regulatory positioning
5. Merchant use cases
