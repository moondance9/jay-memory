# Circle CMP / Agentic Payment

## Current Status
- Circle CPN appears difficult for a Korean entity under the current regulatory interpretation.
- Managed Payments and Agentic/Nano payment use cases remain more feasible discussion tracks.
- GMT should position itself as a KRW settlement, PG, prepaid, and merchant infrastructure partner, not as an unlicensed VASP.

## Key Risks
- CPN-related PR may be difficult.
- Korean regulatory interpretation may limit stablecoin interaction by Korean payment companies.
- Messaging must avoid suggesting that GMT performs crypto exchange, custody, or unlicensed transfer.

## Next Actions
- Define concrete Agentic Payment use cases.
- Separate Korean legal constraints from global stablecoin payment narrative.
- Prepare use cases for Circle product team discussion.
