# GXA Reward Ecosystem

## Current Context
- Galaxia Coin (GXA) is operated by Galaxia Metaverse (GMV).
- GMV receives GXA business operation outsourcing compensation from Galaxia SG.
- Strategic goal is to position GXA as a reward instrument in the stablecoin payment era.
- MoneyTree App and MoneyClub can be used as reward and marketing channels.

## Strategic Direction
- Do not position GXA as speculative investment.
- Emphasize reward, utility, loyalty, and ecosystem collaboration.
- Link GMT stablecoin payment PR with GXA reward ecosystem carefully and legally.
